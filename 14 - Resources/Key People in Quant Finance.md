# Key People in Quant Finance

The people who built quantitative finance. Know their work.

---

## Legends

| Person | Affiliation | Contribution |
|--------|-------------|--------------|
| **Jim Simons** | Renaissance Technologies | Greatest quant trader ever. Medallion Fund: ~66% annual returns (before fees) for 30+ years. Mathematician turned trader. |
| **Harry Markowitz** | UCSD | Modern Portfolio Theory (1952). Nobel Prize 1990. |
| **Fischer Black** | Goldman Sachs | Black-Scholes formula, Black-Litterman model. Died 1995 (no Nobel). |
| **Myron Scholes** | Stanford/LTCM | Black-Scholes. Nobel 1997. LTCM blowup 1998. |
| **Robert Merton** | MIT/LTCM | Continuous-time finance, Merton model. Nobel 1997. |
| **Eugene Fama** | U Chicago | Efficient Market Hypothesis, Fama-French factor models. Nobel 2013. |
| **William Sharpe** | Stanford | CAPM, Sharpe ratio. Nobel 1990. |
