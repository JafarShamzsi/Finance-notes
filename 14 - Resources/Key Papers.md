# Key Papers

Foundational academic papers every quant should know. Organized by topic.

---

## Market Microstructure

| Paper | Authors | Year | Key Contribution |
|-------|---------|------|-----------------|
| **Continuous Auctions and Insider Trading** | Kyle | 1985 | Lambda (price impact), informed vs uninformed |
| **Bid, Ask and Transaction Prices** | Glosten & Milgrom | 1985 | Information asymmetry in spreads |
| **High Frequency Market Making** | Avellaneda & Stoikov | 2008 | Optimal market making with inventory risk |
| **Optimal Execution of Portfolio Transactions** | Almgren & Chriss | 2000 | Optimal execution framework, VWAP/TWAP theory |
| **Empirical Market Microstructure** | Hasbrouck | 2007 | Comprehensive microstructure analysis |

## Portfolio Theory

| Paper | Authors | Year | Key Contribution |
|-------|---------|------|-----------------|
| **Portfolio Selection** | Markowitz | 1952 | Mean-variance optimization |
| **Capital Asset Prices** | Sharpe | 1964 | CAPM |
| **Global Portfolio Optimization** | Black & Litterman | 1992 | Bayesian portfolio construction |
| **Common Risk Factors in Returns** | Fama & French | 1993 | Three-factor model (MKT, SMB, HML) |
| **A Five-Factor Asset Pricing Model** | Fama & French | 2015 | Five-factor model |
| **On Persistence in Mutual Fund Performance** | Carhart | 1997 | Momentum factor (UMD) |

## Statistical Arbitrage & Pairs Trading

| Paper | Authors | Year | Key Contribution |
|-------|---------|------|-----------------|
| **Co-Integration and Error Correction** | Engle & Granger | 1987 | Cointegration testing (Nobel 2003) |
| **Pairs Trading: Performance of Relative Value** | Gatev, Goetzmann, Rouwenhorst | 2006 | Empirical pairs trading analysis |
| **Statistical Arbitrage in the US Equities Market** | Avellaneda & Lee | 2010 | PCA-based stat arb |

## Momentum & Mean Reversion

| Paper | Authors | Year | Key Contribution |
|-------|---------|------|-----------------|
| **Returns to Buying Winners and Selling Losers** | Jegadeesh & Titman | 1993 | Cross-sectional momentum (the original paper) |
| **Time Series Momentum** | Moskowitz, Ooi, Pedersen | 2012 | Time series momentum across asset classes |
| **Does the Stock Market Overreact?** | De Bondt & Thaler | 1985 | Long-term reversal |
| **When Are Contrarian Profits Due to Overreaction?** | Lo & MacKinlay | 1990 | Short-term mean reversion |

## Machine Learning in Finance

| Paper | Authors | Year | Key Contribution |
|-------|---------|------|-----------------|
| **Empirical Asset Pricing via Machine Learning** | Gu, Kelly, Xiu | 2020 | Comprehensive ML comparison for asset pricing |
| **Building Diversified Portfolios that Outperform OOS** | López de Prado | 2016 | Hierarchical Risk Parity (HRP) |
| **The Triple Barrier Method** | López de Prado | 2018 | Better labeling for ML in finance |
| **Deep Learning for Financial Applications** | Heaton, Polson, Witte | 2017 | Autoencoders and deep learning in finance |

## Risk Management

| Paper | Authors | Year | Key Contribution |
|-------|---------|------|-----------------|
| **Value at Risk** | JP Morgan (RiskMetrics) | 1996 | VaR framework |
| **Coherent Measures of Risk** | Artzner et al. | 1999 | CVaR as coherent alternative to VaR |
| **A New Approach to Modeling Systemic Risk** | Brunnermeier & Pedersen | 2009 | Liquidity risk and margin spirals |
| **Optimal Leverage** | Kelly | 1956 | Kelly criterion for position sizing |

## Options & Derivatives

| Paper | Authors | Year | Key Contribution |
|-------|---------|------|-----------------|
| **The Pricing of Options and Corporate Liabilities** | Black & Scholes | 1973 | Black-Scholes formula |
| **Theory of Rational Option Pricing** | Merton | 1973 | Extended Black-Scholes, dividend adjustment |
| **A Closed-Form Solution for Options with Stochastic Vol** | Heston | 1993 | Heston model |
